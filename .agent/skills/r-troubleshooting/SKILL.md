# R Troubleshooting

- Capture the exact command and full error message.
- Check `sessionInfo()`, `packageVersion()`, and `str()` or `names()` for the data.
- Disambiguate conflicts with `pkg::fun()`.
- Consult `docs/troubleshooting.md` for known issues and fixes.
